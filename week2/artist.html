SOMBR

Sombr (real name Shane Michael Boose), born on July 5, 2005, is an American singer, songwriter, and producer from New York City. He began making music as a child, experimenting with GarageBand before moving to Logic Pro. Sombr attended Fiorello H. LaGuardia High School as a vocal major but left during his junior year to focus on music. He gained attention with his 2022 viral single “Caroline”, which showcased his emotional lyrics and indie-pop sound. In 2023, he released his first EP In Another Life, followed by his debut album I Barely Know Her in 2025. His songs, including “Back to Friends” and “Undressed”, became major hits on TikTok and streaming platforms. Sombr’s music blends indie rock and alternative pop, often exploring love, heartbreak, and youth with cinematic emotion and heartfelt authenticity.
